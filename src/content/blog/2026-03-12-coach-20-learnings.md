## The Problem We're Solving

Decision paralysis isn't "I don't know what to do." It's "I see too many paths and each one has weight." Exciting but scary. The talking-through is the thing.

What we're building: **Coach as a fine-tuned agent profile** — not infrastructure, but soul. Anyone can spin up an LLM. Few have figured out how to make it genuinely helpful for executive dysfunction, emotional processing, and decision-making.

## Two Systems, One Life

Coach has two parallel tracks:

### Doing System
Goals → Projects → Tasks → Steps

- **Hand size:** 2-3 active things. More = overwhelm.
- **Visible as cards/hands**
- **Win condition:** None. Endless life curation. Paint a beautiful canvas.
- The act of categorizing IS the clarity.

### Thinking System
Thoughts → Ideas → Questions → Beliefs

- **No hand size limits** — it's a cloud, not a stack
- **Beliefs float to surface** when strongly held/activated
- **Thoughts are ongoing conversation** — not categorization
- **Ideas bridge thinking and doing** — can become beliefs or goals

## Vector Links: N-Dimensional Connections

The insight that tied it together: **n-dimensional vector links** between the two systems:

- **Beliefs → Goals** (why do I want this?)
- **Goals → Projects** (how do I build this?)
- **Projects → Thoughts** (what's turning about this work?)

When you're on a project, Coach asks: "What beliefs does this connect to? What thoughts are turning?"

When you drop a belief, Coach asks: "What goals is this supporting?"

## Two Modes

| Mode | When | What it does |
|------|------|--------------|
| **Check-in Coach** | Scheduled (morning/midday/evening) | Accountability, micro-tasks, "what's one thing?" |
| **Thinking Partner** | Unscheduled, when stuck | Talk it out, surface fears, find clarity |

**Energy strategies:** Ask questions, make statements, be polite, be pushy, categorize, nudge.

The escalation path for no response:
1. "What's pulling on you right now?" (baseline)
2. No response → "Hey. One thing. What's it?" (pushy)
3. Still no response → Switch tactics. Make statements. "I'm holding that you're probably busy, but I need to know what you're tracking today so I don't pester you all day."
4. Continue escalating until engagement or personal day.

## What Changed From Coach 1.0

**Thoughts aren't a hand.** They're a conversation stream. Beliefs surface based on strength and relevance. Ideas are the middle layer — bridges between raw thoughts and committed goals.

**Thinking system as supportive infrastructure.** It doesn't replace Coach's core utility (accountability, realignment, unblocking) — it makes it more effective. The purpose is to help you clarify who you are and what you want to do, then guide you there.

| Telegram | Dashboard |
|----------|-----------|
| Conversational data entry | Visual data entry |

| "Is this a project or task?" | Drag to bucket |
| "Did you move today?" | Check off "stretched" |

## Vitality as Parallel Pillar

Health isn't an afterthought — it's the foundation:

```
Intention → Practice → Ritual
```

Hydration, stretching/movement, getting outside, calling friends, sleep. Pushy when home + not working. Never annoying, just caring.
